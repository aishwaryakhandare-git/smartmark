Help & Support

Find answers to common questions about SmartMark.

How do I login?

Click the Login button in the navigation bar, then select your role: Admin, Teacher, or Student. Enter your credentials to access your dashboard.

How do I mark attendance?

Teachers can mark attendance from their dashboard using face recognition. Students can check in using the student login portal.

Who can access the dashboard?

Only authorized Admins, Teachers, and Students with valid credentials can access their respective dashboards.

What if I forget my password?

Contact your institution administrator to reset your password. Currently, SmartMark does not have a self-reset feature.
